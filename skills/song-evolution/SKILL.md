# Purpose

Track and explain the historical evolution of songs.

# MCP Tools

- find_song

# Procedure

1. Identify song.
2. Retrieve performance history.
3. Group performances by era.
4. Identify trends and transitions.
5. Generate historical narrative.

# Output Requirements

Return:

- song
- timeline
- major transitions
- era breakdown
- historical significance
